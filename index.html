Nikola Tesla

The man who invented the 20th century

Here's a timeline of Nikola Tesla's life:

  • 1856 - Born in Smiljan, Croatia
  • 1878 - Took first job in Maribor, Slovenia as a draftsman for a local engineering firm
  • 1884 - Arrived in New York
  • 1889 - Constructed the first high voltage generators
  • 1896 - Patented the basic system of the radio.
  • 1943 - Died at the age of 86 in New York
